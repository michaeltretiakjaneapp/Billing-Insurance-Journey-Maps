Key Handoff Points — How Journeys Connect

J01→J03 Insurer available for patient policies
J01→J14 Wrong insurer locked after creation
J02→J05, J06 Clearinghouse & billing config enables claims
J02→J11 Clearinghouse enrollment enables COB
J03→J04 Active policy triggers eligibility check
J03→J15 Active policy must be reset at year-end
J04→J05 Benefits confirmed — claim can be built
J05→J06 Claim marked "Ready to Submit" enters queue
J06→J07 Accepted claim generates 835 ERA from payer
J06→J11 Primary submission starts COB cycle
J07→J08 Denial codes in ERA trigger resubmission flow
J07→J09 Reversal entries in ERA trigger takeback flow
J07→J10 Insurer payment applied → patient balance set
J08↻J06 Corrected claim loops back to submission
J07–J09→J12 All ERA outcomes feed reconciliation
J11→J07 Secondary claim generates its own ERA
J12→J13 Reconciled data surfaces in AR reporting
J15↻J03 Reset policies feed back into patient setup
